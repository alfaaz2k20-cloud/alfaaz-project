Our Mission

Alfaaz is an informal collective founded by students united by a shared passion for art, literature, and culture. We are dedicated to fostering spaces where creativity meets collaboration and where the rich artistic voices of our valley find expression.

Celebrate Talent

Highlighting diverse local artists and writers through annual events and carefully curated showcases.

Curate Culture

Bridging the gap between artists and commercial spaces through our venture tchāndērvār by placing art in everyday venues.

Inspire Dialogue

Creating vibrant platforms for creative contests, collaborative clubs, and deep literary discussions among peers.
